SCGI 101

Student Creator's Guide to the Internet

Online Course Description

This online course serves as a guide for students in grades 6 through 8 to creating meaningful content on the Internet.

This course will teach students the basics of internet safety and digital citizenship. Then follow up with mini lessons on how to navigate several content creation websites such as YouTube and blogging, and use programs such as Audacity, Open Broadcasting Software (OBS), and video editing.

Getting Started

Read the information below to get started in this online course.

This course is designed to...

Take you through the basics of creating content on the Internet. First you will learn about digital safety and digital citizenship in the Level 1A Course (link on the right).

Next you will learn about software programs that will help you create, edit, and publish your work onto the Internet. (Level 2A).

Then you will learn about different websites that host varying types of content and how each website caters to a specific audience (Level 3A).

Lastly you will learn about to publish the content you created on the previously mentioned software and into the appropriate webiste. (Level 4A).
